What is the Club Clash?

How it works. Why it's different.

01

Team Rosters

Teams of 10 athletes. Separate Men's and Women's competitions. Your gym fields a full squad, not just your fastest few.

02

The Events

Each gender competes in 7 track events total. Six individual races from sprints to distance, then a finale relay to crown the winner.

03

All 10 Utilized

Every athlete on the roster fills exactly one unique event slot. No repeats. Every member of your squad sees the track.

04

Race Roulette

Events are revealed one at a time, pulled at random from a pre-determined pool. Nobody knows what's next. Not even the coaches.

05

Strategy is Key

Teams must select their athlete for each race without knowing what future events will be called. A smarter strategy can beat a faster squad.
