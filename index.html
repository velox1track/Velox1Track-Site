What is Velox 1?

Velox 1 is a winner-take-all, team-based track & field competition powered by our signature Race Roulette format—where every split-second choice matters.

  • One event at a time. Races are drawn randomly and revealed just before the gun.
  • Rapid roster moves. Teams pick their runner on the fly, reacting instantly.
  • One-and-done. Once an athlete runs, they're out for the rest of the round.

Fast. Fierce. Unpredictable.
